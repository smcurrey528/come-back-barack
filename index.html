Rules of the Game

Barack has been enjoying a well-deserved vacation, and now it is time to get Barack to come back! But first, Barack is going to need your help! He is being chased in the streets by Internet Trolls and he gets exhausted every time he steps on "Make America Great Again" hats in the street.

1) As Michelle always says, "when they take the low road, we take the high road," so use the Space Key to jump over the hats and the Right Arrow key to move #Forward.

2) If Barack gets too exhausted, he might need to go back on vacation so be sure to catch the "Liberal Snowflakes" from the sky to give him some energy.

3) Do you think you can do it? Yes, we can! Good Luck, and get Barack to come back!
